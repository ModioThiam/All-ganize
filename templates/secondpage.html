Start with Intention

Set your Goals:

Setting your goals are crucial to making sure you properly focus your efforts, consider prioritization and measure the progress you are making towards accomplishing those goals. Establishing specific, measurable, and attainable goals are important to create a gameplan for your success, and making sure you are able to reach your destination.

Create a To Do List:

To do lists are within the same avenue as goal setting. The main difference is that goal setting is more for establishing long-term objectives, while to do lists are shorter term tasks in more manageable steps. By taking our larger goals and making them into more bite sized portions, we are better able to ensure that we are not overwhelmed, and are able to feel more accomplished with every completed task.
