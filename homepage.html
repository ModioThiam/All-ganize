About 'A'

Welcome to ‘A’, the optimal productivity resource for busy students and professionals! This website will assist those finding themselves swamped with deadlines and due dates. Are you tired of manually entering in all of your important information? We are here to help!! With our platform, easily upload your PDF documents - like a class syllabus - and our technology will pull out the important information from those documents - like the assignment due dates off of your class syllabus! Try it out today, and experience the benefits for yourself! You will be better able to increase your productivity, keep track of your due dates and schedule.
